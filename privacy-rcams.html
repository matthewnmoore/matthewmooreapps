RCAMS Reminder

Privacy Policy

Overview

RCAMS Reminder is designed to help RNLI crew members remember RCAMS-related tasks and reminders.

The app is designed to be simple, private and focused. It does not require an account and does not collect personal information.

Information We Collect

RCAMS Reminder does not collect, store or share personal information.

  • No user accounts.
  • No names or contact details.
  • No email addresses.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No analytics or user profiling.
  • No payment information.

Location Services

If location-based reminders are enabled, location processing is handled on the device for reminder purposes.

Location information is not sent to us, stored by us or shared with third parties.

Notifications

The app may use local notifications to remind users about RCAMS-related tasks.

Notification settings are controlled by the user through iOS settings.

Third-Party Services

RCAMS Reminder does not use advertising networks, tracking SDKs or third-party analytics services.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may occasionally be updated. Any future changes will be published on this page.
