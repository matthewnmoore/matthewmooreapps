Need Help?

Thank you for downloading The Mumbles Raft Race.

We hope you enjoy using the app and supporting this fantastic community event in aid of Mumbles RNLI.

If you've found a bug, noticed incorrect event information, or have an idea for improving the app, we'd love to hear from you.

Common Questions

The countdown appears incorrect

Please ensure your device date, time and time zone are set correctly.

The donation link doesn't work

The donation page is provided by an external service. Please check your internet connection and try again.

Event information has changed

Although we aim to keep information up to date, details may occasionally change after an app update has been released.

I have found an error

Please email us with as much detail as possible, including the device model and iOS version if applicable.
