Common Questions

Location reminders are not working

Please check that Location Services are enabled for the app in iOS Settings.

Notifications are not appearing

Please check that notifications are enabled for the app in iOS Settings.

The app is not updating my status

Try opening the app and pulling down to refresh. Also check that location permissions are still enabled.

I have found a bug

Please email us with details of the issue, including your device model and iOS version if possible.
