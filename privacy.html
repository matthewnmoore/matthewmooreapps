Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The Mumbles Raft Race App

The Mumbles Raft Race app provides public event information for the Mumbles RNLI Raft Race, including event details, countdowns, schedules, sponsors, galleries, safety information and donation links.

Information Collected by the App

The app does not collect, store, share or transmit personal information.

  • No user accounts are required.
  • No names, email addresses or contact details are collected.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No payment information is collected.
  • No analytics or advertising identifiers are used.
  • No tracking is performed.

External Links

The app may open external websites, including RNLI-related pages and donation services. These external websites are not operated by this app and may have their own privacy policies.

Donations

Donations are handled by external third-party donation services. The Mumbles Raft Race app does not process payments and does not receive or store payment information.

Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
