The Mumbles Raft Race

Privacy Policy

Overview

The Mumbles Raft Race app has been developed to provide information about the annual Mumbles RNLI Raft Race. The app allows users to browse event information, view schedules, sponsors, photographs, safety information and access official donation links.

Protecting your privacy is important to us. This app has been designed to minimise the collection of personal information.

Information We Collect

The Mumbles Raft Race app does not collect, store or share personal information.

  • No user accounts.
  • No names or contact details.
  • No email addresses.
  • No location information.
  • No photographs from users.
  • No payment information.
  • No health information.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No analytics or user profiling.

Notifications

The app does not currently send push notifications.

Third-Party Websites

The application may open external websites including official RNLI websites and authorised donation providers.

These websites are operated independently and have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing those policies before providing any personal information.

Donations

Donations are processed entirely by authorised third-party providers.

The Mumbles Raft Race application does not process, receive or store payment details.

Children's Privacy

The application is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may occasionally be updated. Any future changes will be published on this page.
