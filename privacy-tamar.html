Tamar Companion

Privacy Policy

Overview

Tamar Companion is an educational and reference application designed to support RNLI Tamar Class lifeboat crews by providing operational guidance, technical information and training resources.

Your privacy is important to us. Tamar Companion has been designed to minimise the collection of personal information.

Information We Collect

Tamar Companion does not collect, store or share personal information.

  • No user accounts.
  • No names or contact details.
  • No email addresses.
  • No location tracking.
  • No payment information.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No analytics or profiling.
  • No health information.

Training Progress

Training scores, quiz results and user preferences are stored locally on your own device.

This information is not transmitted to us and is not shared with third parties.

External Links

The application may include links to official RNLI resources and other reference material.

Those websites operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

Tamar Companion does not use advertising SDKs, analytics services or user tracking technologies.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may occasionally be updated. Any future revisions will be published on this page.
